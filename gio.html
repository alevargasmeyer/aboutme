// CHAPTER 01

The bet we made.

In 2023, my co-founder and I looked at the pickleball boom and saw a gap. Hotels, resorts, country clubs, and brands all wanted custom-branded paddles to gift, retail, and program — but the existing options were either off-the-shelf with a sticker, or premium-priced custom that took 60+ days. We bet we could deliver pro-grade custom paddles in 15–25 days at quantities from 20 to 1,000. The rest of this page is everything we built to deliver on that bet.

→

The thesis

Pickleball was the fastest-growing sport in America. Hotels and clubs needed branded paddles for guests and members. Existing custom suppliers were slow, expensive, or low-quality. We could be all three: fast, fair, and pro-grade.

★

The promise

Three product tiers (Fiberglass / T300 carbon / T700 carbon). End-to-end customization. Free design mockups. 15–25 day turnaround. Free US shipping. Sample paddles before commit. We delivered on every line.

// CHAPTER 03

How we made it.

We didn't just slap logos on someone else's paddle. I personally sourced and managed 6 manufacturing partners across China. I vetted each factory, ran sample rounds, specced T700 Toray carbon as our flex material, and negotiated MOQs and turnaround windows that no off-the-shelf vendor could match. Quality control happened on every run. The videos below are real footage from our production lines.

6 suppliers. One standard.

Pickleball manufacturing is fragmented in China. I built relationships with six factories so we could match the right tier (Fiberglass / T300 / T700) with the right specialist, run multiple jobs in parallel, and never get locked into a single supplier's queue. 15–25 day turnaround held even at 1,000-paddle volume.
