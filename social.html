// 01 — TREND I CREATED

The "Elf on a Shelf" trend.

Designed the format. Tagged the right person. Watched it go.

★ THE POST I MADE · WHERE IT STARTED

I posted Garcelle. She reposted. Trend goes.

The format I built: a celebrity portrait with a tiny famous figure perched on their shoulder — a play on "Elf on a Shelf." I made the kickoff post on @popculture: Garcelle Beauvais with Pharrell Williams on her shoulder, captioned "You've heard of Elf on a Shelf, now get ready for this..."

Garcelle reposted it to her own timeline. That's when it took off.
— how a trend actually starts

From there, six A-list celebrities self-tagged the page with their own variations — Anne Hathaway, Reese Witherspoon, Zoe Saldana, Elizabeth Banks, Jenny McCarthy, Barbara Corcoran. Combined likes from those celeb posts alone: over 1.6 million.

Then the Today Show with Hoda & Jenna picked the trend up on national TV — featuring the original Garcelle/Pharrell post on-air.
